MS-CMRIS 2019: Multi-sequence Cardiac MRI Segmentation Challenge
in conjunction with STACOM and MICCAI 2019（Aug 13th, 2019, Shenzhen, China）
Participate procedure
1.Read carefully the Terms and Conditions and return the signed Data Use agreement document.
2.We’ll get in touch and then send you back the data link.
3.Send us or submit by a platform your final results (in nii.gz format) from the validation data set.
4.Submit your paper follow the LNCS style according to the main MICCAI conference guidelines.
Important data
Submission deadline: June 12th, 2019
Notification of acceptance: July 3th, 2019
Workshop Camera ready: July 17th, 2019
Workshop Date: (TBA) Oct 13 or 14th, 2019, Shenzhen, China
Review process
For the submitted manuscripts, they will be firstly reviewed by the workshop organizers who will ensure the quality of the paper reached the publication standard. Then each paper will be reviewed by our reviewers. The review procedure will be double-blinded, similar to the MICCAI submissions. Review comments will be sent to the authors for their further consideration of modifications and preparation for the final camera ready copy that will be included in the workshop proceedings.
Paper format
The format should follow the LNCS style according to the main MICCAI conference guidelines, anonymous and no more than 8 pages.  Supplementary materials such as videos are acceptable and encouraged. If the paper had been submitted to the main MICCAI conference, authors may optionally include the reviews and rebuttal in the supplementary materials.
